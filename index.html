Description

In Comet, you play as the exuberant Stella who confronts her fear of the dark to search for her brother. After witnessing a world-changing event, your brother mysteriously disappears in the middle of the night. You embark on a journey to follow his trail and find answers.

Players will crank up their lamp's light as they foray into the dark and travel across an island stirred by stardust magic. The adventure involves solving puzzles, collecting trinkets, querying islanders, and uncovering the secrets of an island fascinated by the Comet.

Features

  • Engaging Narrative: A mysterious disappearance sets you on a path to uncover island secrets
  • Unique Gameplay: Use the Playdate's crank to control your lamp's light as you explore the darkness
  • Puzzle Adventure: Solve various puzzles while exploring the island
  • Collection System: Gather trinkets throughout your journey
  • Rich World: Interact with islanders and discover the mystery of the Comet
  • Distinctive Art Style: Beautiful black and white visuals optimized for Playdate's unique display
